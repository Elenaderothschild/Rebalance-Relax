About me


There is a vast number of practices and principles of manual therapy across the globe. Most of these methods aim to loosen up, realign,detoxify and reset the body to a state of balance, where it can heal itself.


At an early age I have realised that I am drawn to finding methods to improve one's health and to help those around me who suffer from physical and mental ailments. I also discovered that I possess an innate ability to calm down and relax people around me. At the age of 21 I embarked on a journey of self-realization, looking for fullfillment and inner peace.First it led me to a community of healers who taught me about the mysterious culture of Tibetan Buddhism and introduced me to the vast science of natural medicine.I learned techniques that help regulate the energy of biological systems.Soon I realised that I must follow my calling and help people to a healthier,happier life.Years of study and practise followed.


I have developed a unique style of massage with fully personalized approach to reestablish homeostatis in the body.Along with carefully tailored exercise plans and guidance on healthy eating I can aid those who wish to live without illness or injuries. My mission in life is to fuse the advances of modern science with the wisdom of ancient healers, to create the future of preventative healthcare.
