Massage Therapy

Swedish Massage 60 min - £70

Classic holistic massage involving long, gliding strokes, kneading and circular movements on superficial layers of muscles. It promotes relaxation and rejuvenation, increases blood flow and reduces muscle tension.

Deep Tissue Massage 90 min - £95

A thorough full-body treatment aiming to relieve stress and tension. IIt targets deeper layers of muscles and connective tissue. It involves firm pressure and strokes to address chronic muscle tension and knots.This massage aims to release muscle stiffness and improve mobility. It may cause temporary discomfort when working on specific areas of tension. It involves stretches and the use of advanced techniques for greater effect.

Total Tension Relief 120 min - £110

This comprehensive treatment focuses on eliminating myofascial trigger points in all areas of the body while giving a deep sense of relaxation. A combination of techniques, such as Swedish, deep tissue, and stretching may be used to address tension in various muscle groups.

Chair Massage 45min - £55

A chair massage is a targeted massage which typically focuses on the upper body, including the neck, shoulders, back, and arms. Performed fully clothed, making it a quick and effective way to alleviate tension and promote relaxation.

Percussive Therapy 60 min - £65

Full body massage using Theragun Pro by Therabody. Performed through a towel. It involves rapid and targeted pulses of pressure to specific areas of the body. This technique aims to enhance muscle recovery, reduce muscle soreness and improve overall muscle function. The percussive movements stimulate blood flow and help alleviate tension by reaching deep into the muscles. If you are seeking a quick relief for muscle tightness and fatique this is the treatment for you.

Pregnancy Massage 60 min - £65

A wonderful pampering experience for those expecting. It focuses on addressing the unique needs and discomforts associated with pregnancy. This massage is performed with the pregnant woman lying on her side or using support cushions for comfort. Gentle and soothing techniques are used to alleviate common pregnancy-related issues such as lower back pain, swelling, and joint discomfort. Available in the second and third trimesters.
